About Karen Katz

I have enjoyed bringing my love of art and parties together for many years to Metro New Orleans and surrounding communities via my “STUDIO ON THE GO” parties for kids and adults alike. It is rewarding to use my graphic design background/skills in the creation of canvases and crafts for various themed parties, venues and celebrations. It is the experience I’ve gained from teaching ART-ON-A -CART for pre-schoolers, ART IN THE AFTERNOON to grade schoolers, SIP AND BRUSHSTROKES for seniors, and ART CAMP for all ages that allows me to bring “THE FINE ART OF FAMILY FUN” to you, your friends and family.
